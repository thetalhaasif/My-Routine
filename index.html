Principles
The rules that run this system
Mindset
🏃
Marathon, not a sprint
1.5 hrs of DS learning every weekday morning = 375 hrs/year. More than any bootcamp. Small daily blocks compound dramatically. Don't burn out in month one.
🤲
Tawakkul + action
Do your best work, then leave the results to Allah. This removes anxiety from the equation. Pray Istikhara before big decisions about your career and hustle.
⚡
Freelancing first
Start with freelancing — faster cash, direct ROI on your DA/DS skills, builds confidence. Run personal branding in parallel at lower effort. Reassess in 3 months.
🧠
80/20 your DS learning
Focus on Python, SQL, scikit-learn, and one LLM framework (LangChain or LlamaIndex). Deep beats broad in interviews and with clients. Resist tutorial hopping.
✍️
📝
Content from real work
Your best content: "I solved X at my job using Y." LinkedIn posts documenting your DS/AI learning build audience and personal brand without extra research time.
😴
Sleep is sacred
6.5 hrs minimum (9 PM to 3:30 AM). Every great morning block depends on this. Never trade sleep for a task — you'll underperform at everything the next day.
📊
Track one metric per goal
DS: projects completed/month · Freelancing: proposals sent + revenue · Content: posts published + followers. Simple tracking beats complex systems every time.
🛑
Honor diminishing returns
If you're too tired to produce quality work, it's more effective to stop and rest than to force subpar output. Exhausted side hustle work costs you more than it earns.
